Aam Ras

Aam ras is a delicious Indian dessert made from ripe mangoes. It's essentially a smooth mango pulp served chilledand flavored with cardamom or saffron. Here's a simple recipe to make aam ras at home:

Ingridents:

  • 3-4 ripe mangoes (preferably Alphonso or any sweet variety)
  • Sugar,to taste (optional, depending on the sweetness of mangoes)
  • Milk and icwe cream as per taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom or a few saffron strands (optional, for flavor)

Instructions:

1.

Wash and peel the ripe mangoes. Cut the flesh away from the seed and discard the seed.

2.

Place the mango flesh in a blender or food processor. If the mangoes are fibrous, you can strain the pulp after blending for a smoother texture.

3.

Blend the mango flesh until you get a smooth puree. If desired, you can add a little water to facilitate blending.

4.

Taste the mango puree and add sugar if needed to adjust the sweetness. The amount of sugar required will depend on the sweetness of the mangoes.

5.

If using, add ground cardamom or saffron strands to the mango puree for flavor. Mix well.

6.

Once the mango pulp is ready, transfer it to a serving bowl or individual serving glasses.

7.

Cover the bowl or glasses with plastic wrap and refrigerate the aam ras for at least 1-2 hours or until chilled.

8.

Before serving, give the aam ras a quick stir. Garnish with chopped nuts or mint leaves if desired.

Your aam ras is now ready to serve! Enjoy it as a refreshing dessert or snack.

Ice-cream

Making homemade ice cream can be a fun and rewarding activity. Here's a basic recipe for vanilla ice cream:

Ingridents:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

Prepare the Ice Cream Base:

1.

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar until the sugar is dissolved. Add the vanilla extract and mix until well combined.

Chill the Mixture:

2.

Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight.

3.

Chilling the mixture will improve the texture of the ice cream.

Churn the Ice Cream:

4.

After chilling, pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.

5.

This usually takes about 20-25 minutes.

6.

The ice cream will start to thicken and freeze during churning.

Transfer and Freeze:

7.

Once the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to a freezer-safe container.

8.

Smooth the top with a spatula, cover the container with a lid, and freeze the ice cream for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

Scoop the ice cream into bowls or cones and serve immediately. Optionally, you can garnish with toppings such as chocolate sauce, chopped nuts, or fresh fruit.

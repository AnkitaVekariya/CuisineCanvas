Ice Gola

Ice Gola, also known as Gola Ganda or Chuski, is a popular street food in India, especially during the summer months. It's made by shaving ice and flavoring it with colorful syrups. Here's a simple recipe for making Ice Gola at home:

Ingredients:

  • Ice cubes
  • Ice Gola maker or blender
  • Assorted flavored syrups (such as rose, khus, lemon, orange, etc.)
  • Chaat masala (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Chopped fresh fruits (optional, for garnish)
  • Chopped nuts (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

1.

Crush the ice cubes using an Ice Gola maker or a blender until you get finely crushed ice.

2.

Take the crushed ice and pack it into a cone-shaped mold or onto a stick, pressing it firmly to hold its shape.

3.

Drizzle the assorted flavored syrups generously over the packed ice, covering it from all sides.

4.

Sprinkle some chaat masala and/or lemon juice over the flavored ice for an extra kick of flavor (optional).

5.

Garnish with chopped fresh fruits and nuts if desired.

Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing taste of homemade Ice Gola!

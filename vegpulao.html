Veg Pulao

Here's a basic recipe for making Vegetable Pulao, a flavorful and aromatic rice dish cooked with vegetables and spices:

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup basmati rice, washed and soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, cauliflower, etc.), chopped
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise (adjust to taste)
  • 2-inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)1 teaspoon coriander powder

Instructions:

1.

Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan or pressure cooker over medium heat.

2.

Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

3.

Add bay leaves, whole cloves, black peppercorns, green cardamom pods, and cinnamon stick. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

4.

Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another minute.

4.

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.

4.

Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

4.

Add mixed vegetables and stir to coat them with the spices.

Serve hot with raita (yogurt dip) or any side dish of your choice.

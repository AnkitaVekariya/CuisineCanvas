Chaas is a refreshing drink made from yogurt (curd) that is seasoned with spices and herbs. Here's a simple recipe for making chaas:

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 2 cups chilled water
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt (kala namak), or regular salt to taste
  • A pinch of black pepper powder
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves, chopped (optional)
  • A few ice cubes, for serving (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1.

In a mixing bowl, whisk the plain yogurt until smooth.

2.

Gradually add chilled water to the yogurt while whisking continuously until well combined.

3.

Add roasted cumin powder, black salt, black pepper powder, and chopped mint leaves to the yogurt-water mixture. Stir well to combine.

4.

Taste the chaas and adjust the seasoning according to your preference by adding more salt or spices if needed. If desired, add a few ice cubes to the chaas to make it extra chilled.

5.

Pour the chaas into serving glasses. Garnish the chaas with chopped fresh coriander leaves for a decorative touch.

6.

Serve the chaas immediately as a refreshing drink to accompany your meal.

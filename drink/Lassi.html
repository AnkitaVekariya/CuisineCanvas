Lassi

Lassi is a popular Indian yogurt-based drink that is both refreshing and nutritious. It comes in various flavors, with sweet lassi being the most common. Here's a simple recipe to make sweet lassi at home:

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup chilled water
  • 2-4 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice cubes, for serving (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves or rose petals, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1.

In a blender, combine the plain yogurt, chilled water, sugar, and ground cardamom (if using).

2.

Blend the mixture on high speed until smooth and frothy.

3.

Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if needed.

4.

If you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add more chilled water and blend again until well combined.

5.

Once the lassi reaches your desired consistency and sweetness, transfer it to serving glasses.

6.

If desired, add a few ice cubes to each glass to make the lassi extra chilled.

7.

Garnish the lassi with fresh mint leaves or rose petals for a decorative touch.

8.

Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if needed.

Serve the sweet lassi immediately and enjoy its refreshing flavor!

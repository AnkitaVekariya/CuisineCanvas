Baigan Bharta

Baingan Bharta is a popular Indian dish made from roasted eggplant (baingan) that is mashed and cooked with spices. Here's a basic recipe for Baingan Bharta:

Ingridents:

  • 1 large eggplant (baingan)
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (mustard oil or any cooking oil of your choice) Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

1.

Wash the eggplant and pat it dry. Apply a thin layer of oil on its surface.

2.

Roast the eggplant over an open flame or on a gas stove. Keep turning it occasionally until the skin is charred and the flesh inside is soft. Alternatively, you can roast it in an oven at 200°C (400°F) for about 30-40 minutes.

3.

Once the eggplant is cooked, let it cool down. Then peel off the charred skin and mash the pulp thoroughly. Set aside.

4.

Add chopped onions and green chilies. Cook until onions turn translucent.

4.

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.

4.

Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.

4.

Add the mashed eggplant and mix everything together. Cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

4.

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.

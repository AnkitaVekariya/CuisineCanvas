Raita

Raita is a refreshing yogurt-based side dish popular in Indian cuisine. It's made with yogurt and various vegetables and spices. Here's a simple recipe for making raita at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated or finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh coriander leaves

Instructions:

1.

In a mixing bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.

2.

Add grated cucumber, chopped tomato, chopped onion, and chopped green chilies (if using) to the yogurt. Mix well.

3.

Season with roasted cumin powder and salt. Mix until everything is well combined.

4.

Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.

Serve chilled as a side dish with biryani, pulao, or any Indian meal.

Sweet-corn

Here's a simple and delicious recipe for making sweet corn that you can enjoy as a snack or side dish:

Ingridents:

  • 2 cups sweet corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon freshly chopped parsley or cilantro (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Instructions:

1.

Cooking the Sweet Corn: If using fresh corn, remove the kernels from the cob. If using frozen corn, thaw it according to the package instructions. Heat butter or olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the sweet corn kernels to the pan and saut√© for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the corn is tender and slightly browned.

2.

Seasoning: Once the corn is cooked, season it with salt, black pepper, and chili powder (if using). Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences. Stir well to coat the corn evenly with the seasoning.

3.

Optional Additions: For added flavor, you can sprinkle freshly chopped parsley or cilantro over the sweet corn. You can also sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the corn for a cheesy touch.

4.

Transfer the seasoned sweet corn to a serving dish.

Serve hot as a snack or side dish. Optionally, serve with lemon wedges on the side for squeezing over the corn for extra tanginess.

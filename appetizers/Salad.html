Salad

Certainly! Here's a simple recipe for a refreshing mixed green salad:

Ingridents:

  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (such as lettuce, spinach, arugula, or kale)
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1.

Wash the mixed salad greens thoroughly and spin them dry in a salad spinner or pat them dry with paper towels. Tear any large leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.

2.

Add thinly sliced cucumber, diced tomato, thinly sliced red onion, sliced black olives (if using), and crumbled feta cheese (if using) to the bowl with the salad greens. In a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper until well combined.

3.

Drizzle the dressing over the salad ingredients in the bowl. Gently toss the salad using salad tongs or clean hands until all the ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.

4.

Taste and adjust the seasoning, adding more salt and pepper if needed. Once the salad is well mixed and seasoned to your liking, transfer it to individual salad plates or bowls.

Serve the mixed green salad immediately as a refreshing side dish or as a light main course.

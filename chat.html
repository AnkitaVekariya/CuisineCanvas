Chat

Chat is a savory and tangy Indian street food snack made with a variety of ingredients such as potatoes, chickpeas, crispy noodles, and chutneys. It's a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Here's how to make Chat at home:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups boiled and cubed potatoes
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup mint-coriander chutney
  • 1/4 cup nylon sev
  • 1/4 cup crispy fried noodles (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt (kala namak)
  • 1/4 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

1.

In a large mixing bowl, combine boiled and cubed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, finely chopped onions, tomatoes, and coriander leaves.

2.

Add tamarind chutney and mint-coriander chutney to the mixture according to your taste preferences.

3.

Season the mixture with chaat masala, red chili powder (if using), black salt, and roasted cumin powder. Mix until everything is well combined.

4.

Garnish the Chat with nylon sev and crispy fried noodles (if using) just before serving.

5.

Serve Chat immediately in individual bowls or plates, garnished with lemon wedges on the side.

Enjoy the savory and tangy flavors of homemade Chat!

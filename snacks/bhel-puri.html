Bhel Puri

Bhel Puri is a popular Indian street food snack made with puffed rice, vegetables, chutneys, and various spices. It's a delightful and tangy snack enjoyed by people of all ages. Here's how to make Bhel Puri at home:

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups puffed rice (murmura)
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 2-3 tablespoons tamarind chutney
  • 2-3 tablespoons green chutney
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt (kala namak)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 cup sev (crispy chickpea flour noodles)
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

1.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice, chopped onions, tomatoes, boiled potatoes, cucumber, roasted peanuts, and chopped coriander leaves.

2.

Add tamarind chutney and green chutney to the mixture according to your taste preferences. Mix well to coat all the ingredients with the chutneys.

3.

Season the mixture with chaat masala, red chili powder (if using), black salt, and cumin powder. Mix until everything is well combined.

4.

Garnish Bhel Puri with sev (crispy chickpea flour noodles) just before serving.

5.

Serve Bhel Puri immediately in individual bowls or plates, garnished with lemon wedges on the side.

Enjoy the tangy and crunchy goodness of homemade Bhel Puri!

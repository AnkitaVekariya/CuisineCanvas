Kadhi

Kadhi is a popular North Indian dish made with yogurt (curd) and gram flour (besan), typically flavored with spices like fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds, and turmeric. It can be served with steamed rice or as a side dish with roti or chapati. Here's a simple recipe to make kadhi at home:

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 3 tablespoons gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnish

4.

Add turmeric powder, red chili powder (if using), and salt to the mixture. Mix well.

Garnish the kadhi with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot kadhi with steamed rice or roti.

Certainly! Cashew chutney is a delicious and creamy condiment made with cashew nuts and spices. Here's a simple recipe to make cashew chutney at home

Ingridents:

  • 1 cup raw cashew nuts
  • 1 tablespoon oil (coconut oil or any vegetable oil)
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped 1/4 cup grated coconut (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste or lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste Water, as needed

Instructions:

1.

Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the raw cashew nuts and roast them until they turn golden brown and fragrant. Remove them from the heat and let them cool down.

2.

In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil. Add chopped green chilies, chopped ginger, and chopped garlic. Sauté for a couple of minutes until they turn aromatic.

3.

Add grated coconut to the pan and sauté for a minute. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down slightly.

4.

In a blender or food processor, add the roasted cashew nuts, sautéed coconut mixture, tamarind paste or lemon juice, and salt.
